Frequently Asked Questions

Is the hotel really "closed" for the winter?

Not at all! We're open year-round. Though some guests do report... unusual occurrences during the off-season. Most guests find this adds to the charm.

Can children bring tricycles in the hallways?

We encourage it! Our hallways are expertly designed for recreational cycling. Watch out for the carpet pattern—it's hypnotizing but safe.

What's in Room 217?

A lovely suite with all modern amenities. We've renovated it extensively. Nothing to worry about. Absolutely nothing.

Is there a blood elevator?

We prefer to call it our "signature art installation." It's purely decorative. Well, mostly. Don't mind the water damage on the hallway carpets.

What's your refund policy?

Once you book, you're with us. There's no leaving. Kidding! Full refunds available up to 24 hours before check-in. Mostly.
